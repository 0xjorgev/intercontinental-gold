About us

Intercontinental Gold and Metals Ltd. is a Next Generation Metals and Mining Company providing leverage to commodity prices, exploration and development success and significant growth potential for our stakeholders. Our physical commodities marketing and trading operations provide insights in global primary supply and demand trends that in turn create a strategic and competitive advantage investment and expansion opportunities on a global basis. The Company generates revenues from the purchases and sales of gold and silver. Cost of sales is measured at the fair value of the precious metals purchased and inventory sold, which is purchased at a competitive discount from licensed artisanal and small gold miners (ASGM) in Latin America (LATAM). ASGM supply supports a sustainable revenue generation model. We are unique being the only publicly listed company servicing the LATAM ASGM market.

Our Services

Logistics

Intercontinental Gold and Metals provides its clients with a comprehensive logistic solution designed specifically for each client. We work exclusively with the world’s leading high security transportation and logistics providers to ensure that our clients receive their orders on time.

Assaying

Intercontinental Gold and Metals refinery is equipped with a brand new state-of-the-art assay laboratory that provides trustworthy assays. The ample range of services include chemical assay, fire, and spectrometric. All material arriving at the refinery are weighted by a precision scale before they are melted and sampled by our team of experts. Our laboratory is in the process of being certified as conforming to the general competency requirements that meet the standards of the International Organization for Standardization.

Refining

Intercontinental Gold and Metals operates a state-of-the-art licensed precious metal refinery with the highest standards of quality. Our refinery has an installed capacity that exceeds a half a million ounces of gold and once hundred and seventy five ounces of silver. Our processes include wet chemical chlorination and electrolysis for silver refining. We are proud to have a resourceful team of chemical engineers and metallurgist that add twenty five years of combined experience to our refining process.
